Remote Work Setup & Central Time Requirements

Critical: LabConnect expects professional remote work capability with "camera-on meeting" culture. Your setup and Central Time availability are key hiring factors.

Technical Setup Requirements

  • Professional Video Quality: HD webcam, good lighting, professional background or backdrop
  • Audio Quality: Noise-canceling headset or quiet environment with clear microphone
  • Internet Reliability: Stable high-speed connection for video conferencing and Salesforce work
  • Dedicated Workspace: Distraction-free home office area suitable for confidential clinical trial discussions
  • Security Considerations: Ability to maintain HIPAA-compliant work environment at home

Central Time Zone Expectations

  • Core Hours: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM Central Time (mandatory overlap with Johnson City team)
  • Meeting Culture: Expect frequent video calls with camera on; team uses collaborative approach
  • Flexibility: Occasional early/late calls for international clinical sites coordination
  • Availability: Responsive during business hours for urgent system issues

Interview Preparation

  • Be prepared to discuss your home office setup and how you maintain focus

Contract-to-Hire Success Strategy

Mindset: Approach this as a paid interview, not a temporary role. Your goal is permanent conversion within 3-6 months.

First 30 Days: Foundation & Quick Wins

  • System Assessment: Complete comprehensive Salesforce health check documenting current state
  • Quick Automation: Identify and implement 2-3 high-visibility process improvements
  • Relationship Building: Meet all stakeholders, understand pain points, establish communication rhythm
  • Learning Mode: Absorb clinical trial terminology, regulatory requirements, and LabConnect-specific processes
  • Documentation: Create detailed notes on findings and recommendations for larger initiatives

60-Day Checkpoint: Demonstrating Value

  • Measurable Results: Present data quality improvements, automation time savings, user adoption metrics
  • Strategic Thinking: Propose 90-day roadmap aligned with LabConnect's growth plans
  • Cultural Integration: Actively participate in team meetings, offer solutions, demonstrate remote work excellence
  • Proactive Communication: Weekly status updates highlighting wins and identifying future opportunities

90-Day Performance: Conversion Case

  • Business Impact: Quantify contributions to data accuracy, system efficiency, and user satisfaction
  • Future Value: Present detailed plans for continued Salesforce optimization supporting LabConnect's growth
  • Clinical Competency: Demonstrate growing understanding of clinical trial operations and regulatory requirements
  • Conversion Discussion: Initiate conversation about permanent role, benefits, and continued career development

Key Success Metrics

  • Data quality scores improved by 15-20%
  • Manual process time reduced by 25-30%
  • User adoption increased by 20%+
  • Zero system downtime or critical issues
  • Positive feedback from multiple stakeholders
  • Completed clinical trial-specific training/certification
