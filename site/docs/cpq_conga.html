CPQ & Conga in Trials

CPQ Patterns

  • Products: per-subject/visit fees, monitoring, site start-up costs.
  • Milestone pricing: screened→enrolled→completed visit; tiered per site/volume.
  • Approvals: exceptions and country-specific constraints.

Conga Patterns

  • Composer templates for ICFs and site contracts with version/country clauses.
  • Contracts for redline tracking and version control; workflows to route approvals.
  • Grid for multi-site updates after protocol amendments.
