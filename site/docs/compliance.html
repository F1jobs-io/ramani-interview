21 CFR Part 11 & ALCOA+

Part 11 Controls (Admin View)

  • Identity: unique users, MFA for signers; session controls.
  • Electronic signatures: capture user, timestamp, intent (approve/review), render signature declaration; approval history retained.
  • Audit: Field History Tracking on critical objects; Setup Audit Trail; Event Monitoring/Field Audit Trail (if enabled).
  • Data controls: validation to prevent back-dating; change control SOP; sandbox UAT with IQ/OQ/PQ evidence and trace matrix.

ALCOA+

Attributable, Legible, Contemporaneous, Original, Accurate, plus Complete, Consistent, Enduring, Available. Enforce via validation, flows, restricted edits, and robust auditability.
